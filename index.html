About Japan

Japan became one of the tourists' favorite destination for many good reasons. The country offers a wide range of big cities as well as rural area, while mixing modern and traditional cultures. Whether you're interested in geek culture, entertainement, food or beautiful spots to take pictures, you will find something to do in the Land of the Rising Sun.

What cities to visit in Japan?

Tokyo

Shibuya

The bustling capital of Japan, Tokyo combines ultra-modern and traditional styles in a mix of bright neon skyscrapers and ancient temples.

Kyoto

Fushimi Inari Taisha

Former capital of Japan, Kyoto is famous for its many classical Buddhist temples, as well as its gardens, imperial palaces, Shinto shrines and traditional wooden houses.

Osaka

Osaka Castle

Osaka is a large port city and a commercial center. It is known for its modern architecture, its nightlife and the quality of its street food.

What to eat?

Sushis

The most iconic Japanese dish, made out of seasoned rice and seafood.

Ramen

A noodle soup dish consisting of wheat noodles, a savoury broth, and various toppings.

Okonomiyaki

Hiroshima's famous salty pancake, including batter, sliced cabbage and other ingredients.
